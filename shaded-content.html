Dear Lord, we pray that Your guiding hand would rest upon Compassion. Please continue to bless and direct the steps of this ministry that seeks to bring the hope of Your Son, Jesus Christ, into the lives of children living in poverty. We ask that You open the eyes and ears of the ministry leaders and embolden them to do all that is necessary to make Your name a source of great hope for people around the world who need to hear the good news. Holy Father, I ask that You would draw nearer to me and that Your Holy Spirit would guide thoughts and my ways. I pray that I would have a deeper understanding of Your love and how Your heart – the heart of a loving Father – cares for me and for all people, equally.
